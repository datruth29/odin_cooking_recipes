Simple Bread

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons of dried yeast
  • 2 teaspoons of sugar
  • 1 - 1 1/3 cup of warm water 105℉
  • 1 teaspoon of fine sea salt (alternatively, regular salt works as well)
  • 3 - 3 1/2 cups of all-purpose flower
  • 1 egg

Tools

  • 1 Mixing Bowl
  • 1 Loaf Bread Pan

Instructions

  1. Combine yeast, water, and sugar into a bowl
  2. Stir until it is dissolved
  3. Combine salt, flour, and egg
  4. Mix until the dough is rougher
  5. Take out of the bowl until a lightly floured surface.
  6. Kneed the dough for about 4-5 minutes until the dough is much firmer. It should be able to maintain a shape (here is a youtube link showing how to kneed dough for reference.)
  7. Shape it into a ball and place it into the bowl. Cover the bowl.
  8. Let the dough rise for about 1 to 1 1/2 hours.
  9. Take the dough out of the bowl. Shape and place it into the pan.
  10. Let it sit in the pan for an additional hour (make sure to cover it.)
  11. While this is happening, preheat the oven to 425℉. Make sure to place the rack to the lowest section.
  12. Once the bread has finished resting, place it into the oven for 45 minutes.
  13. At the end of the 45 minutes, take it out the oven and let it sit for 15 - 30 minutes.
  14. You should now be able to cut the bread and consume!
